AirTag Engravings

Back to the Apple Portal

On various Apple products, like the AirTags, AirPod Cases, Apple Pencils, iPads, and iPod Touch, you can get a free engraving when ordering. This engraving can include letters, numbers, symbols, and a selection of emoji. AirTags can contain 4 characters, or 3 emojis depending on the byte count.

Below are all the one-character AirTag engravings you can order on the US website. There are other characters allowed on the websites for other countries, for example, you can engrave Kana on the Japanese site. Everything on this page is about the US site.

Scroll down for more information!
