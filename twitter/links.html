Aesthetic Bio Links for Twitter

Back to the Twitter Portal

This page has a list of special links you can add to your Twitter bio. They have Unicode characters as top level domain names / domain extensions.

If you want to generate your own, click here and follow the instructions.

If you don't want any text and only a symbol, replace "replaceme" with "www".

The preview on the left is what the link ends with, and the URL on the right is what you need to put into Twitter. Have fun with your new aesthetic layout!
