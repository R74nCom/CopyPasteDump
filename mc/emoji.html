Minecraft Bedrock Secret Emojis

Back to the Minecraft Portal

Minecraft has secret text symbols available on Bedrock Edition. You can copy and paste them into chat, signs, or anywhere else with the Minecraft font.

They are actually Private Use Area Unicode characters used internally for game controls, mechanics, and icons. The control icons are only available on the platform they're made for, for example, Xbox controller buttons are only available on the Xbox version of Minecraft.

Copy the character in the 3rd row. It should look like an invalid character until you paste it into Minecraft Bedrock.
