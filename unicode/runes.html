Runes / Runic Unicode Script

⬅︎ Text Symbols

This is a collection of Runes / Runic Unicode characters.

They are used for the representation of text written in Elder Futhark, Anglo-Saxon runes, Younger Futhark, Scandinavian medieval runes and early modern runic calendars. Works by J. R. R. Tolkien also use this script.
