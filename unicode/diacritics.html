Diacritics / Accent Marks

⬅︎ Text Symbols

Diacritics, also known as accent marks, are small marks that are added to letters to indicate tone or pronunciation. They are used in many languages, including English. Many of them are considered combining characters. The Dotted Circle ◌ symbol is sometimes used to represent a letter for the diacritic.

Below is a list of all diacritics in Unicode for you to copy and paste.
