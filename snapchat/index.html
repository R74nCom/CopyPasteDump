Snapchat Portal

Snapchat is a mobile multimedia messaging app in which you can send disappearing texts to friends.

Emoji Meanings

Snapchat displays different emojis next to friends' names if they meet a certain requirement. Here are all the meanings of the default emojis:

🌟 - replayed a snap in the past 24 hours
💛 - #1 best friends with each other
❤️ - #1 best friends with each other for 2 weeks
💕 - #1 best friends with each other for 1 month
👶 - brand new friend
😎 - having a similar best friend
😬 - same best friend
😏 - best friend of them, but they are not best friends for you
😊 - best friend of yours, but you're not best friends with them
🔥 - Snapstreak, Snapped every day for an amount of time
💯 - 100-day Snapstreak, Snapped every day for 100 days
⌛️ - Snapstreak about to end
🎂 - birthday
