Wordle & Clones

When you complete a daily Wordle game, you are given the option to copy-and-paste your results as colored square emoji art, without revealing the answer to others.

This page archives the format of these emoji art pasteables, including similar games, sometimes called "Wordle clones".

Wordle

The first line contains the game's name, along with the day and your amount of guesses out of 6. An asterisk (*) denotes that the user was playing on Hard Mode.

The emojis denote letter guesses and placements, with green meaning that the letter is in the correct spot, and yellow meaning it is in the wrong spot. A blank square means that the letter guess was incorrect, being black if the user is on Dark Theme, and white if not.
