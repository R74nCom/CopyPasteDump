What are ZWJ sequences?

Instead of wasting space in the Unicode standard, some emoji are actually comprised of multiple others, bound together by a sort of 'glue' character called a zero-width joiner. It is typically invisible on its own, but certain emoji will be rendered differently when in one of these sequences.
