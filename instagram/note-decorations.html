Colored Instagram Notes

Back to the Instagram Portal

Instagram Notes are temporary posts that appear above the Direct Messages page. They last 24 hours and can contain text, photos, or music. You can share them with followers that follow back or your Close Friends list.

Some Easter egg words, phrases, or emojis can change the background color of a Note. These are called Note Decorations. Many are limited-time and may not work in the future. These are secret, but this page lists all the known variations!
