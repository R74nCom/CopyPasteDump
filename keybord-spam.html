Keyboard Spam

People tend to spam keys on the keyboard when they are bored. This can be done in many ways, such as typing letters in a specific order or arrangement, or randomly typing keys. Randomly typing keys is known as keysmashing, which can show laughter or shock.

You may have even found this page while doing this yourself on Google. This page will show you some ways that you can spam letters on the keyboard when you are bored.
