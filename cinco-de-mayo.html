Cinco de Mayo

This is a collection of copy-and-paste text symbols and emojis for the Cinco de Mayo holiday, which translates to "Fifth of May", corresponding to its date. It commemorates the Mexican Army's victory over the French Empire at the Battle of Puebla, on May 5, 1862
