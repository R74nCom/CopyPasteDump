How It Works

Use this text tool to quickly convert between one case to another. Simply type or paste text into the first box, and the selected case will appear in the second box for you to copy.

Change the case by using the dropdown list under each text box.

If you forgot to turn off Caps Lock when writing something, you can convert your Uppercase to Lowercase.

Many capitalization methods are supported, including lowercase, UPPERCASE, Title Case, Sentence case, camelCase, snake_case, kebab-case, aLtErNaTiNg cAsE, rANdOm cASe, and oPPOSITE cASE.
