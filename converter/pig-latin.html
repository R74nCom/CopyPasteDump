How It Works

Use this text tool to quickly convert English to Pig Latin. Simply type or paste English into the first box, and Pig Latin will appear in the second box for you to copy.

Pig Latin is a joke language that follows three simple rules:

  • If the word starts with a consonant, move the consonant to the end of the word and add "ay".
  • If the word starts with two consonants, move the consonant cluster to the end of the word and add "ay".
  • If the word starts with a vowel, add "-way" to the end of the word.

You can use this to learn Pig Latin or just to be silly.

Some variants of Pig Latin end vowel words with "-yay" or "-hay" instead, which you have the option to do!
