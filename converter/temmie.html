How It Works

Use this text tool to quickly convert English to Temmie's language in Undertale. Simply type or paste English into the first box, and Temmie text will appear in the second box for you to copy.

This translator is great for rolepaying as Temmie (Tem) from Undertale, who has a strange way of speaking.

For example, Temmies say things like "hOI!!! am TEM".

Except for Bob, he speaks fluent English.
