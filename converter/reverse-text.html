How It Works

Use this text tool to quickly reverse, mirror, or flip your text. Simply type or paste text into the first box, and flipped text will appear in the second box for you to copy.

This can be used to talk in a secret code, or to make your friends flip their phone to read your message.

You can choose to just reverse the position of the characters. You can also mirror or make the text upside-down using a special font, or reverse the order of multiple lines.
