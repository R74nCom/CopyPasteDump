How It Works

Use this tool to quickly convert plain boring text to cool vaporwave text. Simply type or paste text into the first box, and your wide text will appear in the second box for you to copy.

This converter uses characters known as fullwidth characters. They are used to represent Latin letters, numbers, and punctuation in Chinese, Japanese, and Korean.

Fullwidth text is also used online to match the aesthetic of vaporwave music, which you can use in titles and social media posts.
